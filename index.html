GAME OVERVIEW

PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds (PUBG) is a battle royale game that redefined the gaming landscape. Developed by PUBG Corporation, it thrusts players into intense survival scenarios where up to 100 participants parachute onto an island and compete to be the last one standing.

LAND. Parachute into a sprawling battleground, carefully selecting your drop zone to plan your strategy wisely. Whether it’s a high-loot area or a quieter spot, your choice can shape your game.

LOOT. Scavenge through towns, buildings, and crates to gather weapons, armor, health kits, and other essential supplies. Efficient looting not only equips you for combat but also ensures you’re ready for unexpected challenges.

SURVIVE.Adapt to the shrinking safe zones, engage in tactical combat, and make calculated moves to outlast opponents. Only through sharp strategy, teamwork, and skill will you rise as the ultimate victor on the battleground.

WIN. often referred to as securing the "Chicken Dinner," is achieved by being the last player or team standing on the battleground. It requires a blend of sharp strategy, precise aim, and smart decision-making throughout the match.
